Comprehensive 2025 Sprint Plan with Weekly Breakdown

Q1: Laying the Foundation

January

Week 1

  • Activities:
    • Introduce new hires to the team and tools.
    • Conduct kickoff meetings to set 2025 goals.
    • Audit 2024 campaign performance.

Week 2

  • Activities:
    • Finalize strategic roadmap for 2025.
    • Define KPIs and reporting cadence for campaigns.
    • Begin preliminary segmentation in HubSpot.

Week 3

  • Activities:
    • Collaborate with team leads to outline Q1 campaigns.
    • Review AI tools for potential integration.
    • Develop content templates for webinars and emails.

Week 4

  • Activities:
    • Finalize onboarding of tools and new hires.
    • Run internal tests for HubSpot automation workflows.
    • Prepare for February content execution.

February

Week 1

  • Activities:
    • Create content for planned campaigns (blogs, emails).
    • Set up performance tracking dashboards.

Week 2

  • Activities:
    • Refine content drafts with feedback.
    • Launch initial email campaigns to test segmentation.

Week 3

  • Activities:
    • Conduct A/B testing for email subject lines and content.
    • Host a dry run of webinar content.

Week 4

  • Activities:
    • Analyze early campaign results and make adjustments.
    • Finalize preparations for March execution.

March

Week 1

  • Activities:
    • Launch campaigns for Q1 webinars and social media pushes.
    • Monitor performance metrics in real-time.

Week 2

  • Activities:
    • Analyze campaign performance and identify trends.
    • Refine content delivery based on engagement data.

Week 3

  • Activities:
    • Scale successful campaigns to additional audiences.

Week 4

  • Activities:
    • Consolidate Q1 performance metrics and lessons learned.
    • Prepare Q1 review presentation for leadership.
