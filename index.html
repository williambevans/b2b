Exhaustive 4-Week Sprint Plan

Week 1: Orientation and Audit

Day 1: Welcome and Onboarding

  • Activities:
    • Meet the team (Marketing, Product, Data, Content, Sales).
    • Review the company mission, current products, and customer profiles.
    • Familiarize with tools and workflows (HubSpot, Altos, analytics platforms).
  • Roles:
    • Marketing Director (You): Lead introductions and start learning workflows.
    • HR/Onboarding Specialist: Provide access to tools and resources.
    • Team Leads (Content, Product, and Sales): Share an overview of department goals.

Day 2: Systems Walkthrough

  • Activities:
    • Deep dive into HubSpot workflows, automation, and reporting.
    • Review content strategy and existing email campaigns.
    • Begin compiling a list of quick wins (e.g., outdated or underperforming content).
  • Roles:
    • Marketing Director: Conduct the walkthrough.
    • HubSpot Specialist: Provide workflow insights.
    • Content Lead: Assist with campaign reviews.

Day 3: Data Analysis Kickoff

  • Activities:
    • Analyze recent campaign data for performance metrics (CTR, open rates).
    • Identify trends and bottlenecks in lead nurturing and automation.
    • Start auditing partnerships and paid media performance.
  • Roles:
    • Marketing Director: Lead the analysis.
    • Data Analyst: Share insights from analytics platforms.
    • Paid Media Specialist: Provide ad campaign data.

Day 4: Customer Journey Mapping

  • Activities:
    • Collaborate with the product team to map user journeys through the funnel.
    • Identify areas of friction and potential drop-off points.
    • Brainstorm solutions for smoother transitions between stages.
  • Roles:
    • Marketing Director: Guide brainstorming.
    • Product Manager: Share product insights.
    • UX/UI Specialist: Map the journey visually.

Day 5: Strategy Alignment

  • Activities:
    • Present findings from the week to leadership.
    • Set initial 30-day goals and metrics for success.
    • Begin planning the content calendar for Week 2.
  • Roles:
    • Marketing Director: Present findings and lead planning.
    • Leadership Team: Provide feedback and alignment on goals.

Week 2: Strategy Formulation

Day 6: Planning Content and Campaigns

  • Activities:
    • Create a content calendar for the next 60 days.
    • Outline quick-win campaigns (e.g., new email marketing sequences).
    • Analyze webinar and podcast performance for engagement improvement.
  • Roles:
    • Content Lead: Draft the calendar.
    • Marketing Director: Approve and finalize strategies.
