Essential Software Tools for the Team

  • HubSpot - CRM and marketing automation.

    • Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for HubSpot Campaign Management

    Objective

    This SOP provides a detailed breakdown for the Marketing Director to manage and execute HubSpot campaigns effectively. It ensures campaigns align with strategic objectives, incorporate detailed workflows, and deliver actionable insights to leadership.

    I. Campaign Setup in HubSpot

    1. Define Campaign Goals in HubSpot

    Step 1: Collaborate with Leadership

    Meet with leadership to define specific campaign goals and finalize objectives and KPIs.

    Step 2: Establish KPIs

    Define measurable KPIs that align with HousingWire's strategic goals. Examples include:

    • Conversion Rates: Measure the percentage of visitors who complete desired actions, such as form submissions or content downloads.
    • Engagement Metrics: Track metrics like click-through rates (CTR), email open rates, and time spent on landing pages.
    • ROI: Calculate the return on investment for each campaign by comparing revenue generated to costs incurred.
    • Subscriber Growth: Monitor the increase in newsletter or premium content subscriptions.
    • Event Attendance: Measure the number of registrants and participants for webinars or live events.

    Ensure KPIs are tracked and regularly updated within HubSpot’s analytics dashboard for real-time monitoring and reporting.

    Step 3: Create Campaign Framework

    Follow these steps to set up the campaign framework in HubSpot:

    1. Navigate to: Marketing > Campaigns > Create Campaign in HubSpot.
    2. Input Campaign Details:
      • Campaign Name: Provide a clear, descriptive name for the campaign (e.g., "2025 Spring Market Insights").
      • Description: Outline the purpose, target audience, and key objectives of the campaign.
      • Budget Allocation: Document the approved budget for ads, content creation, and other resources.
    3. Link Assets: Associate all relevant campaign assets such as emails, landing pages, forms, and social posts using the Associations feature.
    4. Set Tracking Parameters: Use HubSpot’s URL builder to create UTM parameters for accurate performance tracking.

    Ensure all information is saved and reviewed for consistency before proceeding to the next step.

    Navigate to Marketing > Campaigns > Create Campaign in HubSpot. Input campaign details, descriptions, and budget allocation.

    2. Build Campaign Assets

    Step 1: Identify Required Assets

    Develop all required campaign assets, including emails, landing pages, ads, and social media posts.

    Developing Campaign Assets for HousingWire

    As part of HousingWire's strategic initiatives, the following guidelines outline how to create essential campaign assets, including emails, landing pages, ads, and social media posts. These assets will support our mission to engage audiences, drive subscriptions, and promote premium content.

    1. Email Campaigns

    a. Templates

    Use approved HousingWire-branded templates in HubSpot > Marketing > Email. Ensure the design aligns with HousingWire’s premium content standards.

    • Include subject lines focused on the housing industry.
    • Personalize salutation (e.g., [First Name]).
    • Include clear, actionable CTAs (e.g., "Download the Report" or "Register for the Webinar").

    b. Email Sequences

    • Promotional Emails: Announce upcoming webinars, new research, or gated reports.
    • Reminder Emails: Follow up with contacts who haven’t completed actions.
    • Thank-You Emails: Send post-event follow-ups with resources or next steps.

    c. Testing and Optimization

    • Perform A/B testing on subject lines, CTAs, and email visuals.
    • Review analytics in HubSpot to improve open rates, CTR, and conversions.

    d. Automation and Scheduling

    Automate sequences for reminders, thank-yous, and nurturing workflows. Schedule emails at optimal times based on audience engagement data.

    2. Landing Pages

    a. Design and Build

    Use HousingWire’s approved templates in HubSpot > Marketing > Landing Pages.

    • Headline: Address the value proposition clearly.
    • Content: Highlight the benefits of the offer.
    • Forms: Use progressive profiling to capture essential information.
    • CTA: Use action-oriented buttons such as “Get the Report” or “Join the Webinar.”

    b. Optimization

    • Optimize pages for SEO using HubSpot’s built-in tools.
    • Include meta descriptions, alt text for images, and relevant keywords.

    c. Testing and Finalization

    • Test different versions of headlines, form lengths, and CTAs.
    • Review landing pages with leadership for approval before publishing.

    d. Tracking

    Ensure all landing pages are linked to their respective campaigns in HubSpot for accurate tracking.

    3. Ads

    a. Copy and Visuals

    • Ad Copy: Develop concise, industry-focused copy addressing audience needs.
    • Visuals: Use HousingWire-branded designs with high-quality graphics.

    b. Platforms

    • Google Ads: Target high-intent keywords related to mortgages and housing trends.
    • Facebook and Instagram Ads: Focus on visually engaging formats that encourage clicks.
    • LinkedIn Ads: Target industry professionals with premium research content.

    c. Tracking and Budgeting

    • Add UTM parameters to ad links to measure performance in HubSpot.
    • Allocate budgets based on platform ROI and audience reach.

    d. Performance Monitoring

    Use HubSpot and platform analytics to track ad performance. Adjust targeting or creative elements based on real-time feedback.

    4. Social Media Posts

    a. Draft Content

    • LinkedIn: Share insights and data-driven posts.
    • Twitter: Focus on concise updates and hashtags.
    • Facebook: Foster engagement with highlights from events or reports.
    • Instagram: Use stories, carousels, and visual summaries of research or webinars.

    b. Scheduling

    Create a content calendar in HubSpot > Marketing > Social to ensure posts align with campaign milestones. Schedule posts to go live at peak engagement times.

    c. Visuals and Branding

    • Use Canva or Figma to design on-brand visuals for each platform.
    • Ensure consistency in fonts, colors, and logos to maintain HousingWire’s professional image.

    d. Monitoring and Engagement

    Monitor engagement metrics in HubSpot and respond promptly to audience interactions.
